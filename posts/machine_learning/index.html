logistic function $$ h_\theta=g(\theta^{T}x)\
g(z)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-z}} $$

single training cost function $$ Cost(h_\theta(x),y)= \begin{cases} -log(h_\theta(x))\quad if,y=1\
-log(1-h_\theta(x))\quad if,y=0 \end{cases} $$ Note: y=0 or 1 always

simplifies: $$ Cost(h_\theta(x),y)=-ylog(h_\theta(x))-(1-y)log(1-h_\theta(x))\
\
if,y=1\quad Cost(h_\theta(x),y)=-log(h_\theta(x))\
if,y=0\quad Cost(h_\theta(x),y)=-log(1-h_\theta(x))\
$$ 极大似然法
